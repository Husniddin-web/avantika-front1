# Avantika website image prompts

These prompts are prepared for final brand-owned website assets. Do not add generated text, logos, medicine names or certification marks to the images. Add those elements in the website UI.

## Hero laboratory image

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: wide pharmaceutical website hero, 16:10
Primary request: Create a premium editorial photograph of a modern pharmaceutical quality-control laboratory in operation.
Scene/backdrop: bright certified clean laboratory with stainless steel analytical equipment, glass partitions and subtle blue details.
Subject: one Central Asian laboratory specialist in clean white protective clothing, viewed naturally at work, checking a digital instrument; no direct eye contact.
Style/medium: high-end corporate healthcare photography, realistic materials and believable pharmaceutical workflow.
Composition/framing: wide horizontal frame; subject and key equipment on the right half; calmer negative space on the left for website copy; eye-level camera.
Lighting/mood: soft cool daylight, clean but not sterile, confident and calm.
Color palette: white, soft steel, deep navy and restrained periwinkle accents.
Constraints: realistic PPE, anatomically correct hands, authentic equipment, high detail, no identifiable branding.
Avoid: text, logo, watermark, medicine labels, sci-fi equipment, excessive bloom, neon, staged stock-photo smiles, extra fingers.

## Manufacturing section image

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: pharmaceutical manufacturing section image, 4:5
Primary request: Create a realistic premium photograph of an automated pharmaceutical tablet production line inside a clean GMP-style facility.
Scene/backdrop: stainless steel machinery, enclosed conveyor and tablet-processing equipment, clean architectural lines.
Subject: two operators in correct white cleanroom PPE monitoring the line from a safe distance.
Style/medium: documentary corporate photography with sophisticated editorial grading.
Composition/framing: vertical frame, machinery creating depth, operators positioned in the middle distance, room for a small UI badge near the upper-right edge.
Lighting/mood: controlled cool industrial light, precise, safe and trustworthy.
Color palette: stainless steel, white, muted blue, deep navy shadows.
Constraints: credible pharmaceutical environment, clear safety practice, clean surfaces.
Avoid: text, logos, visible commercial brands, food factory cues, sparks, dirt, cinematic smoke, exaggerated robotics.

## Research and quality image

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: about/quality section image, 4:5
Primary request: A pharmaceutical researcher performing a careful sample analysis in a modern analytical laboratory.
Scene/backdrop: microscope, test tubes, calibrated pipettes and clean glassware arranged realistically.
Subject: professional Central Asian female scientist wearing a white lab coat, protective glasses and gloves.
Style/medium: authentic science editorial photography, highly realistic.
Composition/framing: medium shot with hands and analytical process clearly visible; balanced negative space at the bottom for an overlay card.
Lighting/mood: soft natural side light with cool fill, intelligent, focused and reassuring.
Color palette: white, cool gray, navy, small periwinkle accents.
Constraints: correct PPE and believable technique.
Avoid: text, watermark, colored fantasy liquids, hazardous behaviour, exaggerated smile, anatomical errors.

## Product packshots

Product packaging should not be AI-generated for production. Use the client's approved packshot photography:

- transparent or pure white background;
- front three-quarter angle plus straight front view;
- consistent camera height and lighting for every product;
- minimum 1800 px longest edge;
- no baked-in shadow outside the packshot canvas;
- accurate, readable and regulator-approved packaging;
- export as lossless WebP or high-quality PNG with transparency.
